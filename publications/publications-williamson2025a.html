Long-term trends in global flowering phenology

    Abstract

    The timing of flowering indicates how nature responds to climate change. We used a machine learning model to assess flowering on 8 million herbarium specimens so we could see how flowering timing has changed over hundreds of years. We found high variation in flowering shifts at tropical latitudes, likely reflecting trends in both temperature and precipitation seasonality.
